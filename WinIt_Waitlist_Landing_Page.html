We Have Honest Answers

Everything you need to know before you join. We believe in being completely transparent.

Is this actually real? How do I know it's not a scam? +
100% real. WinIt is a registered business operating under Lagos State Lotteries and Gaming Authority (LSLGA) regulations — License No. [LSLGA-XXXX]. All payments go through Paystack, which is licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN). Every draw result is published publicly so you can verify yourself. We've paid out prizes to winners across Lagos. Check our winner gallery above.
How are winners chosen? Can it be rigged? +
Winners are chosen using a certified Random Number Generator (RNG) — the same technology used by regulated lotteries globally. The draw process is conducted live and recorded. Results are published within 24 hours of every draw with the winning ticket number, so anyone can verify. The system is fully automated and cannot be manipulated by anyone, including WinIt staff.
What happens after I win? How do I get my prize? +
We contact you directly via the phone number you registered with within 2 hours of the draw. Physical prizes (phones, laptops, etc.) are delivered free to your address in Lagos within 5 working days. Cash prizes are transferred to your registered bank account within 24 hours of confirming your win. There are no hidden collection fees — ever.
Is my payment and personal data safe? +
Yes. All transactions on WinIt are processed by Paystack, which uses bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption. WinIt never stores your card details. Your personal information is protected under our Privacy Policy and will never be sold or shared with third parties. We are fully compliant with Nigerian data protection regulations.
What if not enough tickets are sold? Do I get a refund? +
If a raffle does not sell all tickets within 30 days of launch, all ticket holders receive an automatic full refund to their original payment method. No paperwork. No waiting. The refund is processed automatically within 48 hours. Your money is always protected.
Can I buy multiple tickets to increase my chances? +
Absolutely. Each ticket you buy is an independent entry in the draw. Buying 5 tickets gives you 5 chances instead of 1 — a 5x better chance of winning. Early bird members also benefit from bonus tickets: buy 5 and get 1 free, buy 10 and get 3 free. The more you enter, the better your odds.
Why is WinIt only for Lagos State residents right now? +
WinIt is currently licensed under the Lagos State Lotteries and Gaming Authority (LSLGA). Our license currently covers Lagos State residents only. We are actively working to expand to other states. If you're not in Lagos, you can still register your interest and we'll notify you when WinIt launches in your state.
How do I contact WinIt if I have a problem? +
